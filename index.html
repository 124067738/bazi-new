The Origin of BaZi

BaZi (Eight Characters), also known as the Four Pillars of Destiny, is a profound metaphysical system with over 2,000 years of history. It originated in ancient China as a way to map the cosmic energy present at the exact moment of a person's birth.

Each pillar represents the Year, Month, Day, and Hour. By analyzing the balance of the Five Elements (Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, and Water), we can decode the blueprint of your character, wealth potential, and health.
